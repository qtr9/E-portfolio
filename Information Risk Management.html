Information Risk Management 2022J

Module 3

Learning outcomes

    The module aims to provide students with:

  • An understanding of the concepts of information security management.
  • An understanding of the basic principles of information risk management.
  • An understanding of cyber threat and its management.
  • An understanding of the use and benefits of various modelling tools.
  • An understanding of how to create and use security models and assessment tools.
  • An understanding of the relevant standards and legislation which impact risk and security management.
  • An appreciation for current and future challenges, limitations, and opportunities in this field.
  • The ability to present critical arguments for specific actions or outcomes to a diverse audience.
  • The opportunity to reflect on and evaluate their personal development.
